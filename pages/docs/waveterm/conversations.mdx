# Conversations

Waveterm captures the history of your conversations with AI as you work through the terminal.

## Viewing Conversations

You can access your conversation history through the `Conversations` button in the AI walkthrough
tab or access it through the menu: `View → Conversations`.

## Managing Conversations

In the conversations view, you can:

- Rename conversations
- Delete conversations
- Search through conversations

## Conversation History

The conversation history includes all the chats you've had with the AI across all tabs.

This can be helpful when you want to reference previous AI interactions or continue working on a
project that was discussed in a previous conversation.

The conversation history is kept locally on your computer.
