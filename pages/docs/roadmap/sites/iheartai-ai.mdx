# iheartai.ai Roadmap

## Overview

**iheartai.ai** is a community-driven platform celebrating AI innovation, bringing together AI
enthusiasts, practitioners, and learners to share news, showcase projects, and engage in meaningful
discussions about artificial intelligence.

## Domain Purpose

iheartai.ai is designed to:

- **Celebrate AI**: Showcase the excitement and potential of AI
- **Aggregate News**: Curate the latest AI news and developments
- **Showcase Projects**: Highlight community AI projects
- **Build Community**: Connect AI enthusiasts worldwide
- **Inspire Innovation**: Share creative AI applications
